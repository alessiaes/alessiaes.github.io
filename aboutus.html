About us




Welcome to Beats Per Minute, your number one playlist-matching website that connects you with a personalised playlist based on your workout!

Music is a very powerful tool: it can make us laugh, or cry, or pretend we're having a sold out concert in our shower. And it's no surprise that it can also be great for working out! Listening to music while working out has been proven to help you focus and boost your mood, improving your performance and making sports much more enjoyable!

Finding the perfect songs for your workout is all about tempo: our playlists find the perfect songs that match tempo to your heart rate, keeping you pumped up and motivated for the entirety of your workout session! This is how it works: the more intense your workout is, the more upbeat the tempo will be. And if you're going for a less intense workout or a yoga session, we'll get you some nice, slow music that will keep you feeling relaxed and at peace the whole time.

So what are you waiting for? Get yourself workout ready now!
